London

London is the capital city of the United Kingdom. Standing on the River Thames in the southeast of Great Britain, London has been a major settlement for two millennia. Londinium was founded by the Romans.The City of London, London's ancient core − an area of just 1.12 square miles (2.9 km2) and colloquially known as the Square Mile − retains its medieval boundaries.The City of Westminster is an Inner London borough which also holds city status. Greater London is governed by the Mayor of London and the London Assembly.
